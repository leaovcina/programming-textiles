    Historical Influence of the Punchcard

    Looking into the correlation between textile production and technological advancement.


    What started with the automatization of loom weaving with the Jacquard loom and its' operative punchcards to inform of treadle changes, the punchcard system has moved towards popular use in machine knitting, while project drafting for weaving bears resemblance to the binary system used in early computation.

    Technical database and exploration

    How can you use this website?

    First and foremost, this website is intended as a database for technical knowledge to encourage and support craft education from home. Focusing on understanding the design approaches to pattern drafts and punchcards for loom weaving and machine knitting, you can find a variety of techniques within their respective categories in the navigation bar. On the side, an exploration into the historical links of textile production and early computation as a result of the invention of punchcards can also be found under 'Analog Programming'.

    Testing new techniques

    A key component to learning a new technique is learning to implement it independently from theory. Under the weaving and knitting sections you will be able to find interactive pages to create your own designs with no ties to creating an actual piece/sample before feeling confident in the technique.
